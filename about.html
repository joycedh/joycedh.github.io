Hi! I am Joyce a.k.a. joisujoisu, a creative coder/technologist based in Amsterdam. I am interested in researching the intersection between technology or AI and traditional artforms like dance, poetry, visual and performance arts. I am enthusiastic, passionate, creative. I like learning new things or teaching the things I already know to others. I can code in Python, Processing, Touchdesigner, Arduino, and more. I have experience working in the theatre (lights/video/building/creating), operating lights at clubs/festivals, VJing, teaching and I worked as a Data Scientist for a year. I also create art/science works and interactive installations at Studio Zoice.
