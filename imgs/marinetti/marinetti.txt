A reinterpretation of Filippo Marinetti’s Après la marne, joffre visita le front en auto in Pure Data. I wanted to know if it was possible to create poetry with sound instead of words. Marinetti combines sounds with visuals, and Pure Data actually does the same: you create interesting sounds, but the visuals of the program are as interesting to look at as the sounds.
