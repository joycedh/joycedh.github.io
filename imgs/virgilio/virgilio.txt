Together with Berlin artist<a target="_blank" href="https://www.virgiliovogels.com">Virgilio Vogels</a>, I worked on a series of artworks generated by neural networks trained on his previous works and his ideas and visions. The works were showcased, besides one of his new works, in a virtual exhibition hosted by<a target="_blank" href="https://edge-neuro.art">EDGE</a>. The exhibition can still be visited digitally on<a target="_blank" href="https://www.artsteps.com/embed/5fa1a6c4f6b7e90872946013/560/315">Artsteps</a>.
